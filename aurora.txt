
Aurora --

* AWS specific.
* MySQL compatible.
* 5 times better performance than MySQL.


Scaling --

* Start with 10Gb, Scales in 10Gb increments to 64Tb. Storage Autoscaling.
* Compute resource scaling upto 32vCPUs and 244Gb of Memory. Not push button scaling. Manual.
* 2 copies of data in each AZ with min 3 AZ. 6 copies of data in total.
* Self healing storage.

Aurora Replicas --

* 2 Types of Replicas.
* Aurora Replicas -- 15 max.
* MySQL Read Replicas -- max 5.
