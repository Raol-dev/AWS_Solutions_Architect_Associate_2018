

 SNS --
 
* Send notifications.
* Push notifications to Apple, Google, Fire OS.
* Also SMS, email or SQS queues.
* SNS lets you group multiple recipients using topics. A topic is an access point for allowing recipients to dynamically subscribe for identical copies of the same 
  notification. One topic can support deliveries to multiple end points. Like grouping together iOS, Anddroid notifications.
